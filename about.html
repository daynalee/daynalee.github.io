About

Curiosity, Adaptability & People

From living in multiple countries, to working in ad tech and operations, my life and career has always been driven by curiosity, adaptability, and people.

I started my career in healthcare operations during COVID, where I learned what it really means to move fast under pressure, care deeply about people, and build systems that hold up when things get hard.

From there I moved to Seattle to join Uber Eats as a Client Success Operations Specialist, where I helped onboard over 1,000 Postmates merchants following Uber's acquisition — learning how to navigate ambiguity, earn trust at scale, and keep clients moving forward.

At Uber Direct, I shifted into monetization operations, where I owned $16M+ in monthly invoicing, built billing automation from 0% to 96%, and led the recovery of $7M in outstanding credit card debt — work that required equal parts financial rigor and relationship management.

Today I'm a Client Partner at Unity in San Francisco, managing a $40M book of business across ad monetization and user acquisition. I work closely with game publishers and mobile apps to grow their revenue through smarter ad strategies and product adoption.

In 2025, I took a sabbatical to travel across Asia and Oceania — volunteering at a dog shelter in Thailand, joining community-led beach cleanups in Australia, and caring for animals at a sanctuary. It reminded me why I do this work: the people, the connection, the growth.

I'm currently based in San Francisco and open to opportunities in NYC or remote roles (PST or EST).
